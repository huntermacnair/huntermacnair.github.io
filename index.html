ABOUT
HUNTER SCOTT MACNAIR

Hunter is a classically trained actor originally from California's Sonoma Valley.

He has studied with the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, The Barrow Group and Kimball Studio.

He can be next seen as Tybalt in Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet at the Access Theater with The Cradle Theatre Company, from July 17–26 2015.
